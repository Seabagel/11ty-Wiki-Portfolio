## Consistency
Is written as Ratio in ***Water-to-Paint***
- ### Base:
    - **25%** water : **75%** paint
- ### Layer:
  - **50%**, equal ratio water and paint
- ### Wash:
  - **80%** water : **20%** paint. It's essentially Less Pigment, and more Water and Medium
- ### Glaze:
  - **40%** water : **60%** paint
- ### Drybrush:
  - No water, **100%** paint
